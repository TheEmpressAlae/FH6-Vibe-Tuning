## Controller Guardrail

Before judging throttle, gearing, tire bloom, or drift stability, confirm the
Xbox controller rear trigger-stop switches are set to full trigger throw. The
switch physically limits how deeply the trigger can be depressed; middle is
50% throw and bottom is click.
