# 2007 Formula Drift #117 599 GTB Fiorano - AWD Snow 2nd-Gear

## Context

- Game: FH6
- Drivetrain: AWD conversion
- Purpose: compact off-road drift challenge tune with an AWD safety net and
  low-speed tire bloom.
- Controls: Xbox controller, Standard steering, ABS off, traction control off,
  stability control off.
- Shifting: manual, 2nd gear as main scoring gear.
- Status: field-improved hold. First snow/5.00-final test advanced the drift
  challenge by one star.

## Tune

### 1. Tires

- Compound: `Snow Tire Compound`
- Front: `24.0 PSI`
- Rear: `42.0 PSI`

Snow loosened the car more than rally/offroad race tires. The high rear
pressure helped the rear tires bloom before the car built too much road speed.

### 2. Gearing

- Final drive: `5.00`
- 1st: `3.10`
- 2nd: `2.60`
- 3rd: `2.15`
- 4th: `1.72`
- 5th: `1.42`
- 6th: `1.18`

Use 2nd as the main compact-zone scoring gear. 3rd is an exit or recovery gear.

## Field Notes

- Rally tires and the 4.10 final-drive version gained speed too quickly in 3rd.
- The 5.00 final-drive snow version produced a marked improvement and advanced
  the challenge by one star.
- Later controller review found prior runs were affected by the controller
  being set to 50% trigger throw. Full trigger throw is now restored.
