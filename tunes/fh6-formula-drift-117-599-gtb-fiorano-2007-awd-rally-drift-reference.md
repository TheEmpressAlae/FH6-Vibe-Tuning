# FH6 Formula Drift #117 599 GTB Fiorano 2007 - AWD Rally Drift Reference

## Context

- Game: FH6
- Drivetrain: AWD conversion
- Purpose: historical off-road drift reference.
- Status: archived reference. Rally tires had too much loose-surface bite and
  made the car gain speed while holding the tires.

## Tune Snapshot

### 1. Tires

- Compound: `Rally Tire Compound`
- Front: `25.0 PSI`
- Rear: `28.5 PSI`

### 2. Gearing

- Final drive: `4.10`
- 1st: `3.05`
- 2nd: `2.35`
- 3rd: `2.05`
- 4th: `1.70`
- 5th: `1.43`
- 6th: `1.20`

### 3. Alignment

- Front camber: `-5.0 deg`
- Rear camber: `-1.0 deg`
- Front toe: `+1.2 deg` toe-out
- Rear toe: `-0.1 deg` toe-in
- Front caster: `7.0 deg`

### 4. Antiroll Bars

- Front: `7.5`
- Rear: `9.5`

### 5. Springs

- Front: `440.0 lb/in`
- Rear: `420.0 lb/in`
- Front ride height: `8.5 in`
- Rear ride height: `8.2 in`

### 6. Damping

- Front rebound: `5.4`
- Rear rebound: `5.0`
- Front bump: `2.7`
- Rear bump: `2.5`

### 7. Aero

- Front: `55 lb`
- Rear: `20 lb`

### 8. Brake

- Balance: `70% front`
- Pressure: `90%`

### 9. Differential

- Front acceleration: `25%`
- Front deceleration: `0%`
- Rear acceleration: `100%`
- Rear deceleration: `12%`
- Center balance: `78% rear`

## Reference Panel

- Class: `S2 879`
- Braking distance: `87.7 ft` from 60-0 mph and `223.7 ft` from 100-0 mph.
- Lateral Gs: `1.22` at 60 mph and `1.25` at 120 mph.
- Acceleration and speed: `1.924 s` 0-60, `3.303 s` 0-100, `161.3 mph` top
  speed.
- Miscellaneous: `0.51` mechanical balance, `0.47` aero balance, `0.772` aero
  efficiency.

## Field Notes

- The rally-tire version was too effective on dirt.
- 3rd gear climbed to approximately 60 mph on a compact section.
- This reference was superseded by the AWD snow 2nd-gear tune, then by the RWD
  front-end hold revision.
